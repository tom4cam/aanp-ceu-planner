# AANP 2026 CEU Planner

A simple, phone-friendly web app to plan sessions and track CEUs for the **2026 AANP National Conference** (Las Vegas, June 23–27) toward AGACNP board-certification renewal (**75 CE hours**).

## What it does
- **Browse** all 393 real conference sessions. Filter by day, clinical topic/tag, type, content level, free/paid, and recording availability. Search by keyword.
- For each session, choose **Live**, **Watch Later**, or **Skip**; rooms are shown on the real venue floor plan.
- **My Map** lays out live picks by day on the actual floor plans, numbers them in order, and flags far walks between rooms with closer suggestions.
- **Watch Later** queues on-demand sessions for extra CEUs.
- **CEUs** tracks progress toward 75: prior credits + planned live + planned recordings + other CEUs.
- **My Plan** is a concise print-/PDF- and calendar-exportable view of what you plan to attend or watch.
- **My Notes** consolidates per-session notes, presenters, and slide/handout links, with Markdown export.

## Data & privacy
Everything saves in your browser (localStorage). No accounts, no server. Use **Export / Import** to back up your plan or move it to another device.

Session data, rooms, descriptions, presenters, and handouts are derived from publicly available conference data.
